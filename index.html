Privacy Policy – TensifyHero

Developer: Badr Salhi (TrafiGames)
Email: lionlive.com@gmail.com

TensifyHero is an educational English-tenses training app designed to help users improve their grammar through quizzes and flashcards. The app is safe for all ages and does not collect or store any personal data.

1. Data Collection

The app does NOT collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

2. Third-Party Services (AdMob)

The app uses Google AdMob to display banner and interstitial ads.

AdMob may collect anonymous data for ad personalization, such as:

  • Approximate location
  • Device information
  • Advertising identifiers

This data is handled by Google, not by the developer.

3. Advertising & Consent

The app uses Google UMP (User Messaging Platform) to request consent for personalized or non-personalized ads in regions where required (GDPR).

4. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed toward children under 13, but it remains safe and does not collect personal data.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update the Privacy Policy if needed. Any update will be reflected on this same page.
